Core values

There's a reason we do what we do, and a way we've committed to doing it. These make up our core values. We expect them to change and grow as we do, but for now, we think they're pretty accurate.

Community

The dedication, contributions, and success of our community is what keeps us going. Our first and only goal is to help you master your data.

Speed

The only slow-mover at Pilosa is our mascot, Pi. The rest of us work hard and fast to ensure the integrity of our products and the support of our community.

Resilience

We believe that success comes to those who work for it, so we never shy away from a challenge. We will do whatever it takes to solve problems, squash bugs, and build the best products.

Drive

Hard work requires sacrifice. We are willing to go the distance to ensure the success of our projects, our community, and our employees.

Culture & benefits

Our culture is more than what we do, it's who we are.

Covered

Our people are our most important asset. That’s why we offer 100% employer-paid health insurance (medical, dental & vision).

Inclusive

We are committed to creating and maintaining a diverse environment that is friendly to all, including families of all shapes and sizes. We offer 16 weeks paid leave for new mothers, 10 weeks for new fathers, and 10 weeks for adoptive/foster parents.

Flexible

We offer flexible hours and remote opportunities so that our employees can build a schedule that works for them. We also offer unlimited vacation so they can relax, unwind, and explore.

Learning & growing

Knowledge drives innovation. From attending industry conferences to becoming inspirational leaders, we support our employees in their efforts to grow their skills.

Fed

Our snack bar is filled with healthy  goodies, delicious coffee, and all-you-can-drink La Croix.

Fit

We offer fitness rewards, a discounted gym membership, and a new Fitbit for every employee.
